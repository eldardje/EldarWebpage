About me

Building things
that matter.

I'm a Software Engineer and DevOps Engineer based in Arvada, CO. I work across the full stack — from designing responsive frontends in React to architecting cloud infrastructure on AWS.

Currently at Charter Communications as a DevOps Engineer I, I build and maintain scalable AWS infrastructure, automate ETL pipelines with Apache NiFi, and keep production healthy with Datadog monitoring. Before that, I was a Software Engineer at Comcast contributing to the CLEAR Portal — a full-stack internal app used across multiple business units for mentoring and development.

I care about clean, maintainable code, good system design, and building things that actually work reliably in production. I graduated from MSU Denver with a B.S. in Computer Science (GPA 3.64, Provost's Honor Roll).
